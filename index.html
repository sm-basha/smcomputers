About Us

SM Computers & IT Services was established in the year 1996 by young professionals with ethical values to provide comprehensive IT Infrastructure Services and Outsourced Business IT support to growing companies. Our range of services such as Network infrastructure solutions, compute servers infrastructure solutions, storage infrastructure solutions, Remote infrastructure management services, system integration, Virtualization solutions, Building Data centers, and turnkey solutions. As part of our infrastructure services, SM Computers & IT Services helps optimize the performance of your IT Infrastructure, manage end-to-end implementations and provide customized services to help customers build, manage, and optimize their IT Infrastructure. SM Computers & IT Services ensure the quality of the processes and the deliverable of the projects, delivering superior services to clients across different geographies. SM Computers & IT Services is a Platinum Certified partner for HP Enterprise Business in India consecutively for 5 years, which is the highest level of accreditation from HP for those partners who excel in providing Solutions using HPE products and services.SM Computers & IT Services IT Infrastructure Practice provides infrastructure services and solutions that are cost effective, reliable and cutting edge to clients. Our services and solutions help businesses to extract maximum value from their IT investments, increase productivity & efficiency, monitor & manage infrastructure components and take proactive & remedial actions across the IT landscape.Being a customer driven team with strong technical capabilities comprising a team of young certified professionals, we strive to provide the best quality of services by following “Industry Best Practices”.Main focus of SM Computers & IT Services is to provide “Right Solutions” to startups, medium and large corporate to meet the demand of high-end support for both pre-sales and post-sales with our highly-experienced Industry certified professionals
