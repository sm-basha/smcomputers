PRESS RELEASE

Tirupathi: March 18,2016:
It gives us immense pleasure to announce that MLR Auto Ltd. has now become a land mark address in Tirupathi. On Mar 18, 2016, First Auto was inaugurated with state of the art facility Showroom and workshop facility at Tirupathi.
The showroom was inaugurated by Mr. Sundeep Chabbra, Director MLR Auto Ltd. Mr. Lokeshwar Rao, Director MLR Auto Ltd. handed over the keys to first customer. The occasion was also graced by President & CEO Mr. Keshab Sen and key customers, financiers from the city.
